Community Guidelines

Effective Date: June 28, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 28, 2026

SQUADR exists to make playing sports easier and more social. These Guidelines keep the community safe, respectful, and worth being part of. They apply to everything you do on SQUADR — your profile, your sessions, and your chats.

Be Respectful

  • Treat every player with respect, regardless of skill level, age, gender, religion, caste, or background
  • Keep group chat conversations civil and on-topic
  • Disagreements happen — resolve them calmly or step away
  • No offensive language, slurs, or personal attacks of any kind

Show Up

  • If you join or accept a session, be there — ghosting wastes everyone's time
  • If something comes up, cancel early and let the group know
  • Only accept Instant Match requests if you are genuinely available to play right now
  • Repeated no-shows may result in account restrictions

Play Fair

  • Be honest about your skill level in your profile
  • Agree on rules before the game starts
  • Win with humility, lose with grace
  • SQUADR is for recreational and semi-competitive play — keep the spirit friendly

Stay Safe

  • Meet in public, well-lit locations — especially for first-time sessions with new players
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels off, cancel and report
  • Do not share personal contact details in group chats
  • SQUADR is not responsible for injuries during physical activity — play at your own risk

Respect Privacy

  • Do not share screenshots of private chats outside SQUADR
  • Do not share other users' personal information without their consent
  • SQUADR IDs are for connecting on the platform — respect how others use theirs

Rate Honestly

  • Rate players based on actual experience — punctuality, sportsmanship, communication
  • Do not use ratings to settle personal scores
  • Fake ratings are a violation and may result in suspension

Zero Tolerance

The following result in immediate and permanent account termination:

  • Harassment, threats, or intimidation of any user
  • Discrimination based on gender, religion, caste, race, sexuality, or disability
  • Creating fake accounts or impersonating other users
  • Sexual harassment or inappropriate conduct of any kind
  • Using SQUADR to facilitate illegal activities
  • Scamming or defrauding other users

Reporting

If you witness a violation, report it. All reports are reviewed within 48 hours and handled confidentially. SQUADR reserves the right to act at its discretion to protect the community.

Play hard. Play fair. Find your crew.
